About

Unofficial pages/guides/tips about various topics related to GZDoom/Zandronum modding. It's not supposed to be a wiki, but the idea is to keep it simple enough to download and use without internet.

Currently these pages are generated from .md files via custom python scripts. Still working out the details.

It's too early to accept any contributions but whatever. Currently it's pretty much a general .md with html capabilities.
